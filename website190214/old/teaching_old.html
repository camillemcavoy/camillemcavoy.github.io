Teaching

    Outreach

    Caltech Chemistry Club
    Performed chemistry demonstrations for elementary, middle, and high school students. Demonstrations included Roman Candles (fireworks pictured at right), freezing plant matter in liquid nitrogen, water to grape juice to milk to soda (demonstration of a pH indicator, solubility, and gas formation), and more.

    Mentor to Undergraduate and High School Students
    Trained two undergraduate researchers and two high school researchers to clone, express, purify, and characterize cpSRP43 mutants, resulting in co-authorship of these researchers on multiple publications.

    Women in Chemistry Vice President
    Organize and run events for Women in Chemistry at Caltech including breakfasts with Professors, career decision panels, career development seminars, and other informative events.

    Women Mentoring Women and Chemistry Big Sib/Little Sib Mentor
    Mentor to younger graduate students at Caltech; help incoming students to navigate choosing an advisor, deciding on classes, applying to fellowships, and time management.
