Teaching

Biochemistry
Head Teaching Assistant, Caltech Chemistry Department
Instructors: John Richards (2013), Scott Virgil (2015), Judith Campbell and Carl Parker (2016)
•Led a group of three graduate and two undergraduate teaching assistants (TA’s).
•Prepared problem set and exam questions and graded problem sets and exams; organized a weekly recitation and conducted weekly office hours; organized reviews for the midterm and final.

    Outreach

    Caltech Chemistry Club
    Performed chemistry demonstrations for elementary, middle, and high school students. Demonstrations included Roman Candles (fireworks pictured here), freezing plant matter in liquid nitrogen, water to grape juice to milk to soda (demonstration of a pH indicator, solubility, and gas formation), and more. Caltech Chemistry Club Outreach

    Mentor to Undergraduate and High School Students
    Trained two undergraduate researchers and two high school researchers to clone, express, purify, and characterize cpSRP43 mutants, resulting in co-authorship of these researchers on multiple publications.

    Women in Chemistry Vice President
    Organize and run events for Women in Chemistry (WIC) at Caltech including breakfasts with Professors, career decision panels, career development seminars, and other informative events. Edit and update WIC website. Caltech Women in Chemistry

    Women Mentoring Women and Chemistry Big Sib/Little Sib Mentor
    Mentor to younger graduate students at Caltech; help incoming students to navigate choosing an advisor, deciding on classes, applying to fellowships, and time management. Caltech Women Mentoring Women

    Caltech Y Washington DC Science Policy Trip
    The Caltech Y DC Science Policy Trip was a phenomenal opportunity to learn the intricacies of science policy in context in Washington DC. This trip allowed us the opportunity to interface with leaders from many governmental science organizations including NSF, NIH, DARPA, ARPA-E, White House OSTP, AAAS and more. Caltech Y
